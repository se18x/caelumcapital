Expanding Caelum Capital

We started Caelum Capital with backtests and trading models. Now, we’re expanding into a new dimension: analyzing companies, industries, and transactions the way investment banks do. This next step is about taking what we’ve learned in quant and applying it to deal-making — building skills in valuation, strategy, and corporate finance along the way.

Inside Our Deal Pitches

Each pitch deck is designed to mirror professional M&A and capital markets work. We break down potential transactions with clear investment theses, financial modeling, comps and precedents, and integration considerations. Our goal isn’t just to practice — it’s to share how real deals can be understood from the inside out.

Services

Pitch Decks We Build

Professional presentation decks tailored for different capital markets scenarios, designed to communicate complex financial strategies with clarity and impact.

M&A Pitch Decks

We analyze potential acquisitions and mergers, showing the strategic rationale, valuation, and expected synergies. Each deck distills the deal into a clear narrative of why it makes sense and why now.

IPO / Equity Raise Decks

For companies preparing to go public, we craft decks that frame the investor story, market positioning, and use of proceeds. The goal is to showcase growth potential and benchmark against public peers.

Private Equity (LBO) Decks

We evaluate buyout opportunities from a financial sponsor’s perspective, focusing on value creation levers and returns. Decks cover investment thesis, leverage structure, and potential exit paths.
