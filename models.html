CX-01 — SMA Strategy

CX-01 is Caelum Capital’s first fully implemented trading model, designed to explore momentum-based trend detection using simple moving average (SMA) crossovers. This strategy tracks two SMAs — one short-term and one long-term — to capture directional market shifts. When the short-term SMA crosses above the long-term SMA, it signals a potential bullish trend, prompting the model to enter a long position. Conversely, when the short-term SMA crosses below, the model exits the trade to preserve capital.

CX-01 was built to serve as a foundational learning model — testing not just whether the logic works, but how it performs under real historical conditions. The model was validated on over 19 years of SPY (S&P 500 ETF) data, completing 500+ trades. It helped establish core infrastructure for trade execution, position tracking, and signal evaluation — forming the baseline for future model iterations and improvements.
