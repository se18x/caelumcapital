CX-1.1 — Profit-Optimized SMA Strategy

CX-1.1 is Caelum Capital’s second trading model, designed to capture large, long-term market trends through a breakout-driven approach. Unlike CX-01’s frequent trading style, CX-1.1 trades only when broader market conditions confirm a strong bullish environment. The model uses a dual SMA regime filter — requiring the 50-day SMA to remain above the 200-day SMA — to define favorable conditions. Once in a valid regime, CX-1.1 holds a long position until price closes below the 20-day SMA, which acts as a trailing exit signal.

This design makes CX-1.1 a low-frequency, high-conviction model. Instead of generating hundreds of trades, it focuses on a small number of well-timed entries that can last months or even years. Backtested on over 19 years of SPY (S&P 500 ETF) data, CX-1.1 completed fewer than 20 trades, yet delivered significantly higher average profit per trade compared to CX-01. Beyond raw performance, CX-1.1 also tested the team’s ability to handle extended holding periods, monitor drawdowns, and manage unrealized gains — making it both a benchmark for performance and a case study in the trade-off between patience and activity in systematic strategies.
