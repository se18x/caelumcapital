What is Caelum Capital?

Caelum Capital is a student-run quantitative trading project focused on building from first principles. It’s an ongoing effort to explore how real-world hedge funds operate—by coding, testing, and learning hands-on.

Strategy Breakdown

Backtesting Engine

Our proprietary backtesting framework processes historical market data to validate trading strategies before live implementation. We analyze performance metrics, drawdown periods, and risk-adjusted returns across multiple market conditions.

SMA Strategy

Simple Moving Average crossover strategies that identify trend changes and momentum shifts. We optimize parameters for different timeframes and market conditions, balancing signal frequency with reliability.

Trade Logging

Comprehensive trade tracking system that records entry/exit points, position sizes, and performance metrics. This data-driven approach enables continuous strategy refinement and risk management optimization.
